Frequently Asked Questions

Enrolling yourself in one of our programs is the easiest thing ever.

1. How can I enroll in one of the programs?

Enrolling in your favorite courses is as easy as anything. Just search for your favorite course(s), click on the register button, and get enrolled. If you are facing any difficulties, you can also reach out to our admissions department and we will help you out.

2. How would I be trained in the courses?

At Upskillz, we train our students in live classes that are hosted on our very own platform. Every student would be assigned a schedule that mentions the class timings and dates that the students have to follow.

3. How long would I have access to my dashboard?

Every training program on our site has a duration period, that ranges anywhere from 2 months to 6 months, or even beyond in some cases. As long as the course lasts, you will have the access to your dashboard.

4. What happens if I miss a live session?

Every live session gets recorded and automatically sent to your dashboard. This session is available for you to access as long as you are a learner in one of our training programs.
